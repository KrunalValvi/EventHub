Who We Are

EventHub is India's leading online ticketing platform for movies, events, sports, and theatre. Founded in 2020, we've revolutionized the way people discover and book entertainment experiences.

With millions of users across the country, we're committed to making entertainment accessible, convenient, and enjoyable for everyone.
